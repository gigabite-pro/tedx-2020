Speakers

Mr. Sahil Verma

Mr. Sahil Verma is an actor and assistant director of The Viral Fever. He has also written and directed several videos and web series. Some of which are Stags (2018), Flames (2018) and Humorously Yours (2016). He also acted in several videos of Youtube Channel, Timeliners like School Days, Road trip with Dad and many more.

Mrs. Bipasha Lakra

Mrs. Bipasha Lakra is an Assistant Professor and teaches at the Department of Political Science at Jesus and Mary College, University of Delhi. Her research interests are- Indian Politics, Indian Foreign Policy, International Relations, Subaltern Studies, Indigenous Studies and Political Movements in India.
